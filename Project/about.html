    Our Story

    We started our service in 2021 hoping to provide a place to feel and learn about the universe in our daily lives. We run a cafe where customers can see planetarium shows with their tea and sell home projectors. The name "Astraea" came from a goddess in Greek myth. She was the last god to leave human society believing in people until the end and still hopes we will make a peaceful world again. We named the cafe her name with wishes the same as hers.
